Chief Mobile Home Park is a manufactured home community located in Justice, Illinois. Since 1950, the same family has owned and operated Chief and management and staff specialize in the rental and sale of new and pre-owned mobile homes. The mission is to provide exceptional service and a safe, close knit community for the residents.

Community Features:

Professional on-site staff
Off street private parking
City water and city sewer
Public transportation nearby
Close to to major expressways
Pet friendly
Close to Midway airport
Across the street from Wilkins Elementary School
Close to local stores, banks, post office, and parks

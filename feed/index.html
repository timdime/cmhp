The residents of the Village of Justice were asked to show support of law enforcement officers by hanging blue ribbons. Chief Mobile Home Park enthusiastically responded.

“The ribbons play tribute to the men and women who have lost their lives and who continue to serve,” said Fran Mills, Chief’s property manager.

Begun humbly in the 1950s, Chief was a site for pull-in campers. It has evolved into a thriving company that specializes in the rental and sales of new and pre-owned mobile homes.

Third generation family members own Chief Mobile Home Park. They are involved in day-to-day operations and are dedicated to providing an exceptional, stable and close knit community for Chiefâ€™s residents, all of whom care about their homes as well as each other.

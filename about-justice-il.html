About Justice, IL

Justice is a vibrant village in Cook County, Illinois. Known for its peaceful neighborhoods and friendly community, Justice offers a suburban feel while being close to the bustling city of Chicago. Our mobile home park is situated in a prime location, providing easy access to major expressways, making commuting and traveling convenient for our residents.
